About WebTra

WEBTRA the name denotes a major and sophisticated method for medical treatment technology ie WEB treatment.

Why choose us?

Analyzing the current system of medical diagnosis and treatment a patient doctor relationship is limited in the walls of a diagnosis room in a hospital where the patient has to consult the doctor directly each time a minor or major health issue affects them.Even though technological development

has influenced medical diagnosis field a lot it has not got any improvement in the consultation mechanism as mentioned before. To make a change in this situation we can prefer our new system WEBTRA with its full effect of avoiding direct consultation of patient with doctor.

What we do?

we introduce a health conscious system. When a patient consults a doctor in a hospital the patient is added to doctor’s WEBTRA Consultants list and each and every details of the patients health issues and biological parameters needed for analyzing patients health is recorded along with details of medicines preferred medicines provided, health issues faced by patients and further details. These records are under the control of a nurse who can be consulted for the details on need. Also the whole contents are under the management of a hospital administrator who provide the webtra support to the doctors for easy access with the patients.

Advantages

  • Time delay of treatment and consulting doctors can be avoided
  • Paper works can be reduced in a hospital patients details management process
  • Doctors need not spend entire time in hospitals treating or consulting they can do It from anywhere with the assistance of nurses
  • Patients can even consult doctors online without going to hospitals on requirement
  • No need of rescheduling emergency surgeries on absence of doctor.
